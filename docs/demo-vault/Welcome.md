# Welcome to Emend 👋

A quiet place to write. Your notes are **plain Markdown files** on disk — no
database, no lock-in, no account. Open a folder and start typing.

> The best tool is the one that gets out of your way.

## What you can do here

- ✍️ Write in a distraction-free editor with live, *dimmed* syntax
- 👁 See a faithful **preview** side-by-side — code, tables, diagrams, and math
- 🔗 Link notes with `[[wiki-links]]` and embed them with `![[note]]`
- 🔍 Jump anywhere with **Quick Open** (`⌘P`)
- 🤖 Summarise long notes with *your own* AI model — or never touch the network
